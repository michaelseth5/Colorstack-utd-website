Here's What We Covered:
Tech unemployment is at 3.75%, the cloud market is headed to $1.2 trillion by 2028, and 3.5 million cybersecurity roles are still unfilled. The jobs are not disappearing, they are changing.
Use programs like CodePath's Technical Interview Prep (TIP) for structured DSA practice that simulates real interview environments.
Learn Prompt Engineering and get certified through programs like IBM or CodePath. CodePath's Applied AI Engineering course is 10 weeks, 4 hours per week, and teaches you how to build in AI-assisted workflows, write precise prompts, and rapid prototype. You even get complimentary access to Claude Code. This is becoming a must-have skill no matter what role you are in.
Have your six behavioral stories ready and use the CARL method (Context, Action, Result, Learning) instead of STAR. That "Learning" piece is what shows growth and self-awareness to recruiters.
Join organizations like ColorStack, NSBE Professionals, and SHPE not just for networking, but for mentorship, accountability, and community. And don't just join. Lead! That's what makes you stand out.
